About Us!

Our History And Mission

Connecting with Buyers

Brings together artisans, gemologists, and specialized merchants from the provinces of Goma/Gisenyi in the north of Lake Kivu, and Bukavu/Cyangugu in the south.

Our History

GL'OR COOPERAT is a dynamic association of professionals passionate about the world of precious stones. Founded in 2018. Congo/Rwanda.

Collaborative Platform

One of the pillars of our cooperative is to facilitate direct connections between precious stone sellers and buyers.

BEST SERVICES

Our Services:

  • Sale of Authentic Precious Stones

    The cooperative offers a wide range of precious stones, including diamonds, sapphires, emeralds, rubies, and various semi-precious stones. Each stone is certified for its authenticity and quality.

  • Advice and Expertise

    Our expert gemologists are available to provide personalized advice and detailed information on the characteristics and value of precious stones. They help clients make informed choices based on their needs and preferences.

  • Evaluation and Certification

    We offer evaluation services to determine the value of precious stones. Each evaluation is accompanied by a certificate of authenticity issued by independent and recognized professionals.
